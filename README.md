# Telecom Customer Churn Analysis

## Overview
An exploratory data analysis project investigating why customers leave 
telecom providers, using a dataset of 7,032 customers.

**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn  
**Dataset:** IBM Telco Customer Churn (Kaggle)

## Key Findings
- **26.6%** of customers churned — more than 1 in 4
- Month-to-month contract holders churn at dramatically higher rates
- New customers are most at risk — churn peaks in the first 3 months
- Higher monthly charges (avg USD 80) are linked to higher churn

## Business Recommendation
Retention efforts should focus on new customers in their first 3 months, 
month-to-month contract holders, and customers paying above USD 70/month.
